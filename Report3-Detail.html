Economic Benefits

Resource efficiency initiatives have generated annual cost savings of $3.8 million while simultaneously reducing environmental impact. The return on investment for efficiency upgrades has averaged 18 months, demonstrating the strong business case for sustainable resource management.

Industry Leadership

Our resource efficiency performance places us among the top 10% of companies in our sector. We actively share best practices through industry associations and collaborate with peers to drive sector-wide improvements in resource stewardship.

Future Targets

Looking ahead, we have set ambitious targets including achieving 50% reduction in water intensity by 2026, 60% improvement in energy efficiency by 2028, and 90% material circularity by 2030. These goals align with global sustainability frameworks and demonstrate our commitment to continuous improvement.
